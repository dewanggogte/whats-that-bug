---
title: Multiplayer rooms now hold up to 20 players, plus group session creation
date: 2026-06-13
tag: Multiplayer
---
Room capacity raised from 5 to 20. A new **Create Group Session** flow lets you generate 2–4 rooms at once and get all invite links (with QR codes) in one share sheet — useful for splitting a large group into parallel rooms. Hosts can now remove players mid-game, not just from the lobby. Daily challenge players also now see the multiplayer invite after finishing.
